### 🧑‍💻 A Little About Me

I'm a data person at heart — someone who genuinely enjoys the moment a messy spreadsheet or a chaotic dataset finally clicks into a clean dashboard that tells a story. Over the past 3+ years, that curiosity has taken me from writing Python scripts to designing full analytics ecosystems, and I'm still just as hooked on it as when I started.

I sit at the intersection of **business intelligence and software engineering** — I like understanding not just *what* the data says, but *why* it matters to the people making decisions from it.

### 💡 What Drives Me

- 🔍 I like digging past the surface-level number until I find the *actual* reason something broke, dropped, or spiked
- 🧩 I enjoy connecting the dots between raw operational data and the decisions leadership actually needs to make
- ⚙️ I have a soft spot for automation — if a task is repetitive, I probably already tried to script my way out of it
- 📈 Good dashboards feel like good writing to me: clear, honest, and easy to act on
- 🌱 I mentor and enjoy helping others get comfortable with Python and data thinking
